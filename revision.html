1. Genetics & Inheritance

Mnemonic for Mitosis Phases: "I Passed My Anatomy Test" = Interphase, Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, Telophase.

Key Bullet Summaries:

  • Mendel's Laws: Law of Dominance, Law of Segregation (Anaphase I), Law of Independent Assortment (Metaphase I).
  • Incomplete Dominance: Red + White = Pink flowers (1:2:1 phenotypic ratio).
  • Co-Dominance: ABO Blood grouping (both alleles expressed equally).
  • Linkage: Exceptions to Independent Assortment. Crossing over occurs during Pachytene of Prophase I.
Hardy-Weinberg Equation: p² + 2pq + q² = 1 | p + q = 1

(Assumes random mating, no mutation, large population, no selection)

3. Animal Physiology

Heart Blood Flow Summary:

  • Vena Cava → Right Atrium → Tricuspid Valve → Right Ventricle
  • Pulmonary Artery → Lungs (Oxygenation) → Pulmonary Veins
  • Left Atrium → Bicuspid (Mitral) Valve → Left Ventricle → Aorta → Body
Cranial Nerves (Functions): "Some Say Marry Money But My Brother Says Big Brains Matter More"
(S = Sensory, M = Motor, B = Both)
Hormone Source Gland Primary Action
Insulin Pancreas (Beta cells) Lowers blood glucose (Glycogenesis)
Glucagon Pancreas (Alpha cells) Raises blood glucose (Glycogenolysis)
Thyroxine (T4) Thyroid Gland Regulates basal metabolic rate (BMR)
Cortisol Adrenal Cortex Stress hormone, increases blood glucose
